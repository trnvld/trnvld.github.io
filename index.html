Who we are

Blackshield Capital Group provides a unique set of private banking and corporate services, developing and implementing an individual investment strategy for all asset classes according to your objectives and risk profile.

We combine experience, sophistication, connections and a range of investment solutions with the client-centric focus of an independent investment boutique. Our expertise in a broad range of financial activities goes beyond traditional banking and investment services.

Our mission is to protect and increase our clients' capital. Our team of experienced professionals is committed to deliver long-term value to our clients seeking to protect their assets in a constantly changing world through effective asset management.
